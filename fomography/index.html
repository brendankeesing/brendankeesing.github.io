FOMOGRAPHY is a story-driven photography adventure game. Set over the course of an entire lifetime, you play as a boy trying to rediscover and document his memories. What has been forgotten that only the photos can show? What memories do even the photos refuse to share?

Relive your forgotten life to remember what made you into what you are.
Capture memories using the camera your mother has passed down to you.
Configure and customize your camera to get the perfect shot and to represent the real you.
Keep track of your adventure by filling your photo album.
A huge world to explore, with many different places, and secrets to uncover.
Meet the important people throughout your life and learn how they shaped your life, and how you shaped theirs.
